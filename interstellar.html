Storyline

Interstellar is a science fiction movie set in a dystopian future. The movie follows a group of astronauts who travel through a wormhole near Saturn in search of a new home for humankind. The story is set in Earth's near future, where environmental disaster means that Earth will perish within the next century. The movie stars Matthew McConaughey, Anne Hathaway, and Jessica Chastain.
