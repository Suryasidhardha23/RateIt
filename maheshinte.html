Storyline

Mahesh Bhavana, a photographer in the idyllic town of Idukki, leads a peaceful life until a scuffle changes everything. After being humiliated in front of his neighbors, he takes a vow not to wear slippers until he has exacted revenge. What follows is a subtle yet profound transformation in his character. This humorous and heartfelt journey explores personal growth, resilience, and the power of quiet determination.
