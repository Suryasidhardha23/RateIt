Storyline

M.S. Dhoni: The Untold Story is a 2016 Indian biographical sports drama film directed by Neeraj Pandey, based on the life of Mahendra Singh Dhoni, the former captain of the Indian national cricket team. The film chronicles Dhoni's journey from a ticket collector to a world cup-winning captain, highlighting his struggles, achievements, and the personal sacrifices he made along the way. It features Sushant Singh Rajput in the lead role, portraying Dhoni's rise to fame and the challenges he faced in his cricketing career.
