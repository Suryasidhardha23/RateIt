Storyline

Set in the 1990s, Minnal Murali follows Jaison, a tailor from the village of Kurukkanmoola, who gains superpowers after being struck by lightning. As he grapples with his newfound abilities, he must confront Shibu, another individual who has acquired similar powers but uses them for nefarious purposes. The film blends action, comedy, and emotion, presenting a unique take on the superhero genre within a Malayalam cultural context.
